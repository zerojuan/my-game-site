Story

Set during the 2013 Manila Game Jam, experience the jam from the point of view of the game's tabloid-tv obsessed game creator. Product placements, pop culture references, 3 endings and 1 unconventional love story.

Note: The game is written in Filipino and, even with translation, draws heavily on Filipino pop culture.

Artist Statement

48 Hours per Second is a game about the making of the game, 48 Hours per Second.

The work deconstructs the idea that everything can be crafted into a playful experience -- including the crafting process itself.

The intimate nature of a Visual Novel, a game genre typically enjoyed alone in a dark room, is combined with the game's gossipy, personal narrative creating an experience closer to voyeur:exhibitionist than the traditional player:game designer.
